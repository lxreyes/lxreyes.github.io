SKY SIM

2026

A 3D flight simulator. Take off over procedurally generated terrain — hills, water, forests and drifting clouds — with an arcade flight model: throttle, pitch / roll / yaw with coordinated banking turns, lift, drag, gravity, and stall and landing / crash detection. Switch between chase and cockpit cameras, read a full glass HUD with airspeed, altitude and an attitude indicator, all set to procedural engine and wind audio. One self-contained HTML file on Three.js, no build step.

  • Three.js
  • WebGL
  • 3D
  • Web Audio

SALT & PLUNDER

2026

A top-down, open-world pirate game. Click anywhere on the sea — or the minimap chart — to set a course across a procedurally generated 6000×6000 ocean of islands. Sink rival pirate ships and scoop floating gold, then dock at Port Royale to buy bigger hulls, more cannons, and upgrades. Vanilla JS, no dependencies, no build step.

BALL BOUNCE

2026

A neon-arcade timing game. A glowing ball flies between two narrow walls; three small platforms scroll continuously through the ceiling and floor on each side. Tap SPACE the exact moment the ball touches a platform — mistimed or misaligned and it’s game over. Each successful bounce ramps the speed and shrinks the platforms.

NEON VIPER

2026

Snake, reborn as an endless shooter. Slither across a borderless world with a camera that follows the head — there are no walls, only your own tail. Eat orbs to grow and fire lasers straight ahead to shear segments off the rival snakes that hunt you. Get bitten and they steal a segment — lose your last and it’s over. Combo multiplier, golden orbs, off-screen threat arrows, particle bursts, screen shake and procedural audio. One self-contained HTML file, no dependencies, no build step.

CALM

2026

A tiny calm corner of the internet. Pick an ambient sound — rain on a metal roof, a fireplace, the ocean, wind, forest birds or night crickets — and a matching pixel scene drifts past in a first-person walk. Every sound is synthesized live with the Web Audio API and every scene is drawn pixel-by-pixel — no audio files, no images, no tracking. Runs completely offline.

  • Vanilla JS
  • Web Audio
  • Canvas 2D
  • Pixel Art
  • Offline

AUTOCLICKER & MACRO

2026

A macOS desktop utility. The auto-clicker fires the mouse at a configurable interval — left / right / middle, single or double, forever or for a set count. The macro recorder captures real mouse and keyboard input with timing, then replays it at adjustable speed and loop count, and saves to JSON. Global hotkeys (F6 / F7 / F8) drive it even when the window is hidden. Built with Python + tkinter + pynput, with the clicking and macro engines kept separate from the UI.

  • Python
  • tkinter
  • pynput
  • Desktop
  • Automation
